Import Historical Data
Upload CSV files with historical student records (2019–2026)
📋
Upload your CSV files exactly as they are.
The system accepts your original format:
Zone, School, Surname, Name, LIN, Sex, Age, Class
• LIN is your 16-digit learner number — year, EMIS and sequence are extracted automatically.
• Sex (M/F), Age and Class are read from your CSV.
• Columns can be in any order — extra columns are safely ignored.
📂
Click to choose CSV files — multiple files allowed

CSV Format Template

Your original CSV format — upload as-is:

Zone,School,Surname,Name,LIN,Sex,Age,Class
CHOWO,Chowo Primary School,BANDA,CHISOMO,2020165004000001,F,8,STD 1
CHOWO,Chowo Primary School,PHIRI,KONDWANI,2020165004000002,M,9,STD 2

• LIN format: [YEAR][16][EMIS 6 digits][SEQUENCE 4 digits]
• Example: 2020165004000001 = Year 2020, District 16, EMIS 500400, Learner 0001
• Sex must be M or F — Age must be a number — Class e.g. STD 1
